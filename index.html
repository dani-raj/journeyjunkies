The tastes worth taking a long-haul flight

Filipino cuisine is just as rich as its natural scenery. With their influences throughout their history and their traditional cooking techniques, Filipinos tend to combine flavours and make the most of any ingredients. Our trips not only introduce you to the most beautiful places of the country but also to its most delicious meals. Hungry yet?

An underwater world that takes your breath away

Watching David Attenborough documentaries are nice, but why not make your own discoveries? From the giant whale shark to the tiny clown fish, the Philippines never fails to amaze anyone going underwater. It’s also home to one of the few biggest coral reefs.
